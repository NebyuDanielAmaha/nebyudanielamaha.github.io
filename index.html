About

I am a public health nutritionist and epidemiologist whose research focuses on nutritional epidemiology, mental health, women's health, biological aging, physical activity, and antimicrobial resistance. My work uses advanced epidemiologic and statistical methods to investigate determinants of health across the life course using large-scale population datasets including Demographic and Health Surveys (DHS), NHANES, and hospital-based data.
