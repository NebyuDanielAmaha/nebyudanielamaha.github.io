About

I am a public health nutritionist and epidemiologist with research interests spanning nutritional epidemiology, biological aging, physical activity, dementia prevention, women's health, mental health, and antimicrobial resistance. My work uses advanced epidemiologic and statistical methods to investigate determinants of health across the life course using large-scale datasets including Demographic and Health Surveys (DHS), NHANES, UK Biobank, and hospital-based data.

Current Research Areas

Biological Aging

Physical activity intensity and biological aging metrics using NHANES accelerometry data.

Dementia & Precision Health

MRI biomarkers, genetics, machine learning, and dementia risk prediction using UK Biobank.

Nutritional Epidemiology

Maternal and child nutrition, dietary diversity, ultra-processed foods, and health outcomes.

Pharmacoepidemiology

Antimicrobial resistance, prescribing practices, and rational use of medicines.
