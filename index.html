About

I am a public health nutritionist and researcher with expertise in nutritional epidemiology, child undernutrition, ultra-processed food consumption, and antimicrobial resistance. My research focuses on applying large-scale population data to understand determinants of malnutrition and inform evidence-based public health interventions.

Research Interests

Nutritional Epidemiology Child Undernutrition Stunting & Wasting Ultra-Processed Foods Maternal & Child Health Antimicrobial Resistance Pharmacoepidemiology
  • Determinants of child growth failure and anemia in low-income settings
  • Diet quality, ultra-processed food consumption, and child nutrition
  • Antibiotic use, prescribing patterns, and antimicrobial resistance
  • Population-based epidemiological analysis using DHS and survey data
