About

Public health nutritionist and epidemiologist specializing in nutritional epidemiology, mental health epidemiology, women’s health, biological aging, and physical activity research. My work integrates population-based datasets (DHS, cohort and survey data) with advanced statistical methods to investigate life-course health determinants in low- and middle-income settings.
