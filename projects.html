Mental Health Epidemiology

Nutrition & Mental Health Depression Population Health Active Research Area

This research program examines the relationship between dietary exposures, lifestyle behaviors, and mental health outcomes across diverse populations. The focus is on identifying modifiable risk factors that may contribute to depressive symptoms and psychological wellbeing.

Current Work

  • Dietary fibre intake and depressive symptoms before and during COVID-19
  • Nutritional determinants of mental health disparities
  • Population-level risk factors for depression and anxiety

Women's Empowerment and Health

Women's Health Empowerment DHS Analysis Active Research Area

This research program investigates how women's empowerment influences health and wellbeing, with particular attention to mental health, nutrition, reproductive health, and health-seeking behaviors in low- and middle-income countries.

Current Work

  • Women's empowerment and depressive symptoms
  • Women's empowerment and anxiety
  • SWPER domains and mental health outcomes
  • Women's autonomy and nutritional outcomes

Biological Aging

Healthy Aging PhenoAge NHANES Data Analysis

This research program explores determinants of biological age acceleration using biomarker-based aging measures. The goal is to understand how lifestyle, nutrition, and physical activity influence aging trajectories and long-term health.

Current Work

  • Physical activity and biological aging
  • Lifestyle determinants of accelerated aging
  • Biological age acceleration using NHANES biomarkers
  • Epidemiologic approaches to healthy aging research

Physical Activity Epidemiology

Physical Activity Chronic Disease NHANES Data Analysis

This research program evaluates how physical activity and sedentary behavior influence chronic disease risk and healthy aging. Particular emphasis is placed on identifying activity patterns that promote longevity and reduce disease burden.

Current Work

  • Physical activity and biological aging
  • Sedentary behavior and health outcomes
  • Moderate-to-vigorous physical activity and aging trajectories
  • Population-level physical activity recommendations

Nutritional Epidemiology

Diet Quality Child Nutrition Population Health Ongoing Research Program

My broader research program focuses on nutritional epidemiology, including dietary quality, dietary diversity, ultra-processed foods, and child nutritional outcomes. This work aims to inform evidence-based nutrition policy and intervention strategies.

Current Work

  • Ultra-processed food consumption and child nutritional status
  • Diet quality indicators and health outcomes
  • Dietary diversity among children in post-conflict settings
  • Population-level nutritional transitions
